Embroidery

Just Name It is a family owned business, with over 17 years of custom embroidery experience. We cater to individuals, teams, organizations, businesses, and other groups. Whether you need help designing the perfect embroidered logo or already have artwork ready to be printed, we can serve you. We also offer tackle twill capabilities. Ask us for more details! Our talented art department can create logos for you, or we can digitize your logo for a small fee. We also accept already digitized artwork in the PXF file format. Pricing for embroidered items depends on total thread count per garment.

Silkscreen

Silkscreen printing applies a layer of ink on top of the garment fabric. The ink is then heat cured to ensure durability. While silkscreen is ideal for woven natural fabrics, we can print on polyester, nylon, virtually any garment. From one color to full color process printing, Just Name It, can produce your next silkscreen order for sports teams, company events, casual wear, and more. The most cost effective solution for large orders Prints can be any color, on any color and type of fabric, including nylon Durable and long lasting Multiple location printing possible Can produce any size order, with a minimum quantity of 12 pieces per design

Digital Print

Our state of the art digital printing process applies a water-based ink onto a garment to dye the underlying fabric. The ink is then heat cured to ensure durability. This process produces vibrant and vividly detailed prints in full color. We can even print photographs right onto a shirt! Full color printing capability No set up fees or screen charges Does not fade, long lasting Ink binds to the fabric and feels smooth to the touch No limit on the number of items you can order: from 1 to 100+

Sheets of vinyl are fed from rolls onto our plotter-cutter and the design is cut out with the aide of a computer. The plotted vinyl is then heat pressed onto the garment to activate an adhesive to apply the print. Our plotted vinyl applications are perfect for jersey numbers, small orders of one color designs, and personalizing or identifying apparel you already own, such as hats and bags. One color vinyl comes in a gamut of solid colors, including florescent and metallic variants. In addition to our one color vinyl process, we also offer a full-color vinyl application when silkscreen or digital print applications are not suited to your project or preferences. Fast turn around times Convenient and low cost Popular for jerseys, bags, shorts, and other athletic items No minimum quantities or set up charges
